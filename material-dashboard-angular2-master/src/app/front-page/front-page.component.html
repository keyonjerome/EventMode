OUR VISION

We all met for the first time at Hack the Hammer II, and we quickly decided on our project: A way for teens across Ontario to find STEM events. Attending events is crucial for networking, getting inspired, and exposing oneself to a whole new world of ideas and discoveries. It turns out that not every event is marketed on Facebook.com or Meetup.com, so we collected info on nearly 1000 events from different APIs using Python. The frontend was built with Angular, and the email newsletter was made with the Mailgun Node.js API. We hope you find our platform useful!
